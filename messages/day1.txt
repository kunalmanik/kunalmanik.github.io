Dil jahan masoom hai itna
Jismein bass jaye to mann ki shaanti aa jaye,
Judd kar iss masumiyat se
Apna khuda bhi aap mein bass jaye,
intezaar hai uss ghaddi ka
Jab Dil ke saath labz bhi judd jaye,
Khyaalon mein basera to abhi bhi hai
Bas saamna ho iss kadar - ki ruh ki bhi mulakaat ho jaye.
