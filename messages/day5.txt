Wafa ki hai uss dil-e-rooh se
Khafa hai wo khamoshi mere mann se
Mai to uss tann par bhi itna itrata hun
Wo be-saans kapp-kappi hai mere hatheli mein

Nafs ne bhi dil ka dariya badaya hai
Humare darmiya ek naya ehsaas laaya hai
Keh diya tha beintehaan, bina koi lafz zubaan pe
Apne labbon ko iss kadar maine sehlaya hai

Todd di zanzeerein, chal diye khwab-e-jahan mein
Rooth gayi thi kasmein, thehera diye jo ambaar mein
Uss ehsaas ki chaahat mein qasurvaar bhi ban gaya
Kambakht duniya ko bhul gayi, socha nahi uss shareek-e-hayaat ne apnaane mein, mujhe apna banane mein
