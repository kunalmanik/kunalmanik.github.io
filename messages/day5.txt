Wafa ki hai uss dil-e-rooh se
Kafa hai wo khamoshi mere mann se
Mai to uss tann par bhi itna itrata hun
Wo be saans kapp-kappi hai mere haathon mein

Nafs ne bhi dil ka dariya badaya hai
Humare darmiya ek naya ehsaas lekar aaya hai
Kehne ko tha nahi kuch khaas zubaan pe
Labbo ko iss kadar sehlaya hai

Todd di zanzeerein, chal diye khwab-e-jahan mein
Rooth gayi thi kasmein, thehera diye jo ambaar mein
Uss ehsaas ki chahat mein qasurvaar bhi ban gaya
Kambakht duniya ko bhul kar bhi, uss shareek-e-hayaat ne apna bana daala, mujhe apna bana daala
