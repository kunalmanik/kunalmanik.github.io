Narmiyan thi unn haathon ki
Baahon ne pukara tha
Saans bhi surr mein beh rahe the
Lafzon ne nihaara tha

Uss Muntazir ke aage wo sajaa sach tha
Jissne usse pukara tha
Khil uthaa tha jagg saara
Aisa aakhon ne nihara tha

Beet gaye the din-o-raat, sajni ki khoj mein
Sajni ne paas khade hi pukara tha
Jhoom uthaa tha manzar
Jabb uske dil ne mere dil ko nihara tha
